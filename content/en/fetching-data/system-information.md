### Fetch available venues

```js
URI: https://<im4>/venues
```

- **Request-type:** `GET`
- **Parameters:** _none_
- **Return data** `{mixed}` `(JSON)`

Fetches list of all available venues.  
<badge>Since 0.11</badge>
